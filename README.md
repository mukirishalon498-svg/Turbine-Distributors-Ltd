# Turbine Distributors Ltd

Official website for **Turbine Distributors Ltd**, a company specializing in the sourcing, supply, and distribution of high-quality agricultural and industrial commodities.

## 🌍 About

Turbine Distributors Ltd connects suppliers and buyers by providing reliable procurement and distribution services across local and international markets.

Our product range includes:

- 🌾 Rice
- 🍚 Sugar
- 🌽 Grains & Cereals
- 🌻 Edible Oils
- ☕ Coffee
- 🌱 Fertilizers (DAP, UREA, NPK, CAN and more)

## 🚚 Services

- Commodity Sourcing
- Wholesale Supply
- Global Distribution
- Import & Export
- Logistics Coordination
